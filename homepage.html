Project Description
Aim

Main aim

Definition:

Create a website that recommends games for (15-30 years old) users with an AI system based on their preferences, finance, and background within 6 months of the first launch.

Detail

After asking website users to fill out a survey about their wants, the website will subsequently provide game recommendations based on the information they have provided, as determined by the AI system. Our website primarily serves people between the ages of 15 and 30, who are more likely than users of other ages to play video games. The launch of this website is scheduled for sometime in the next six months.

Goal 2
Goal 3

Definition:

Create an extensive game database in the first month.

Detail:

We must build a sizable game database regarding various game genres in order to improve the experience and increase the selection of games for users. Since there are several games, a database is essential for managing all the data, and we may increase productivity with the database. Within a month, we will complete this assignment, including meetings with several game developers to register copyrights and organize the data.

Definition:

Build an AI system within 4 months

Detail:

We aim to create a usable AI system with five properties during the next four months: learnability, memorability, the efficiency of use, error management, and subject satisfaction. AI systems with sound judgment would provide consumers with enormous better options in a brief period of time.

Definition:

Build a professional user-centered design with a minimalistic website interface in 2 weeks.

Detail:

An intuitive website interface aids in converting prospective website visitors into consumers. Because of its many practical characteristics, it improves a website's responsiveness, effectiveness, and accessibility in addition to its aesthetic appeal because of its numerous useful features. Displaying user information and their rights is what it means to be user-centered. For these reasons, we consider a decent user-centered design and website interface to be a crucial objective to accomplish with our website.
